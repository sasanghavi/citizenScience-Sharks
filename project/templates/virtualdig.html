Citizen Science Instructions

Welcome to Citizen Science!

Your goal in this game is to find all of the teeth!

You will scroll around the page, and click on teeth to place them in the collection. You can move left, right, up, and down in your search.

There is a button in the top, right-hand corner of the screen titled Show Collection!. This button will show you how many teeth you have collected.

When you are finished, open your collection, and continue on to measure the teeth you found!
